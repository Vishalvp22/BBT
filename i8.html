Summary: BMW i8 is the world's first Petrol-Electric hybrid supercar. It is powered by a 1499cc turbocharged Inline – 3 petrol engine with 2 hybrid synchronous electric motors. The petrol engine for the car generates a maximum power of 228 bhp @ 5800 rpm with the electric motor generates max. power of 129 bhp. The car generates a maximum torque of 320 Nm @ 3700 rpm with the electric motor generating a torque of 250 Nm. The hybrid supercar runs on a 6-speed automatic petrol engine and a 2-speed automatic electrical motors. The hybrid has a mileage of a whooping 47.45 kmpl with an electric range of 37 kilometers. The car has a ground clearance of 117 mm.
